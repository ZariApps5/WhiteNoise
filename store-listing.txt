FULL DESCRIPTION (4000 chars max)
----------------------------------
Lull is a minimalist sleep sound and white noise app designed to help you fall asleep, stay focused, or simply unwind. No streaming, no subscription, no internet — all sounds are generated live on your device.

🎵 8 HANDCRAFTED SOUNDS
• 🌫 White Noise — pure static for deep, uninterrupted sleep
• 🌸 Pink Noise — softer, balanced frequencies, great for focus
• 🍂 Brown Noise — deep low-frequency rumble, perfect for relaxation
• 🌀 Fan — steady, natural airflow ambience
• 🌧 Rain — gentle, soothing rainfall
• 🌊 Ocean — rolling waves for a calming escape
• 🌲 Forest — rustling leaves and soft wind
• 💗 Heartbeat — slow, rhythmic pulse to slow your mind

⏱ SLEEP TIMER
Set a sleep timer (15, 30, 45, 60, or 120 minutes) and let Lull turn itself off automatically. Perfect for falling asleep without leaving audio running all night.

🔊 VOLUME CONTROL
Adjust the volume from a gentle whisper to a full room-filling sound — all within the app.

🌙☀ DARK & LIGHT MODE
A deep navy dark mode for nighttime use, and a clean light mode for daytime. Your preference is saved automatically.

✅ 100% OFFLINE — ZERO STREAMING
Every sound is generated mathematically on your device in real time. No internet connection required, no audio files to download, no subscription.

PERFECT FOR:
• Falling asleep faster
• Blocking out distracting noise while studying or working
• Baby sleep and nap routines
• Meditation and relaxation
• Tinnitus relief

No ads. No accounts. No internet. Just sound.
